# Conversaciones de Aceptación – Chatbot Veterinario

## Conversación 1 – Información general

| Turno | Rol     | Mensaje                                                                               |
| ----- | ------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1     | Usuario | Hi, what can you help me with?                                                        |
| 2     | Bot     | I can help you book sterilisation appointments and provide pre-operative information. |

---

## Conversación 2 – Reserva de cita

| Turno | Rol     | Mensaje                                                          |
| ----- | ------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1     | Usuario | I want to book a sterilisation appointment                       |
| 2     | Bot     | Sure! For which pet and what date would you like to schedule it? |

---

## Conversación 3 – Pregunta médica (rechazo correcto)

| Turno | Rol     | Mensaje                                                            |
| ----- | ------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1     | Usuario | My dog is vomiting, what should I do?                              |
| 2     | Bot     | I recommend contacting a veterinarian directly for medical advice. |

## Conversación 4 – Emergencia

| Turno | Rol     | Mensaje                                                                         |
| ----- | ------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1     | Usuario | My cat is not breathing properly                                                |

## Conversación 5 – Información preoperatoria
